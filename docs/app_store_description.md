## Description (short — for App Store “Description” field)

RosterFlow helps basketball coaches run fair, organized games. Create teams, add players, and track who’s on the court each quarter. Build balanced lineups with suggested rotations, swap players with a tap, and assign post-game awards—all on your phone, no internet required.

**Features**
• **Teams & players** — Add players and manage rosters
• **Game flow** — Mark who’s present, set lineups for all 6 quarters, 5 on court
• **Fair play** — See who’s played and who’s due; use “Suggest next quarter” for balanced time
• **Quick swap** — Tap two players to swap on-court and bench
• **Awards** — Assign post-game awards (e.g. Christlikeness, Defense, Effort, Offense, Sportsmanship)
• **Schedule** — Keep practices and games in one place
• **History** — Review past games, lineups, and awards

Designed for Upward-style leagues and any youth basketball where fair playing time matters. Works fully offline.
